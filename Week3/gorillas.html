GORILLA

Gorillas are primarily herbivorous, terrestrial great apes that inhabit the tropical forests of equatorial Africa. The genus Gorilla is divided into two species: the eastern gorilla and the western gorilla, and either four or five subspecies.

Mass: Western gorilla: 160 kg, Eastern gorilla: 140 – 210 kg Speed: Western gorilla: 40 km/h, Eastern gorilla: 40 km/h Height: Western gorilla: 1,4 m

Lifespan: 35 – 40 years
